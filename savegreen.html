CASE STUDY

There are 2 opportunities that this application will cater to
A. Recommendation based application to Save Money
B. Recommendation based application to be and buy eco-friendly

Opportunity A U.S. consumer spending has gone up and savings has dropped to a 10-year low. This rate of spending is not sustainable and the lower savings rate is expected to be a major constraint on consumer spending in the future. This gives us an opportunity to not only help increase savings for current consumer spending but in turn help the economy rebuild and grow. In a recent Gallup polls, 65% of Financially stressed population (worried about paying normal bills, worried about maintaining a specified standard of living, don’t have enough to live comfortably) enjoy saving more.

79% of Americans agree that they consider themselves an environmentally conscious person. But roughly three in four people don’t take action beyond recycling and hitting the light switch. Globally, a report from 2014 says that 55% of consumers across 60 countries are willing to pay higher prices for goods from environmentally conscious companies. A 2013 study claims that 71% of Americans at least consider the environment as a factor when shopping. Organic food sales in particular seem to have turned a corner, jumping 11.3% in 2014, while overall food sales grew only 3%. Sales in 2014 were over $35 billion - compared to only $3.4 billion in 1997. Growth is expected to continue strongly until at least 2018. The main goal here is to understand both the opportunities and merge it into an application. The vision of the product is help users in saving money and guide them in buying green/eco-friendly should be problem that needs to be tackled together. The reason being – per analysis (by Trulia, Felipe Chacon) money is a barrier to being environmentally conscious. In addition, most people aspire to do what is best to protect the planet, but there are clear time, convenience and cost limitations. In addition based on Statista’s latest report 35.5M people in the US said they pay more for eco-friendly products and services. We will want to solve this problem thereby people are able to save money and also buy green.
